My biography

     My name is Mukhametzhan Janel Yerlanovna, was born on August 21, 2003 in the village of Presnovka, Zhambyl district, North Kazakhstan region. From 2009 to 2020, she studied at the KSU "Presnovskaya Kazakh Secondary School" in the village of Presnovka. During her studies, she took part in republican subject Olympiads, in the 11th grade she took 1st place in the republican distance Olympiad in physics, at the district stage of the republican subject Olympiad in history she took 2nd place. For academic success in KSU"Presnovskaya Kazakh secondary School" was sent to the republican health camp "Baldauren" in Kapchagai. Participated in the Timur movement, was a presenter of various school events. Since September 2020, a student of Ecol-20k FMEN. I am currently an active student of my faculty and group.
